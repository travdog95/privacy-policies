Score Skull King - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 12, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how Score Skull King ("App"), developed by Travis Peters ("Developer"), collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information from you. All data used in the App, such as game scores and player names, are stored locally on your mobile device and are not shared with the Developer or any third party.

Use of Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely for the purpose of facilitating gameplay within the Score Skull King App. This includes:

  • Keeping track of game scores and player names.
  • Providing statistics and historical data for your games.
  • Personalizing your experience within the App.

Disclosure of Information

The App does not share any information with the Developer or any third parties. Your information is stored securely on your device and is not accessible to anyone else.

Data Retention

The data stored locally on your device is retained for as long as you choose to keep it. You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the App.

Security

The Developer takes reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Therefore, while the Developer strives to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, the Developer cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

The Developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated Privacy Policy will be posted on the App and will be effective immediately upon posting. The Developer encourages you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Your Consent

By using the App, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
